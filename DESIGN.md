# Design

## Direction

Light, high-energy, illustration-led community site. Probe B is the composition contract: dominant rainbow hero, practical ice-blue schedule band, text-led membership, and a generous community-story finish.

## Tokens

:root {
  --canvas: oklch(1 0 0);

## Type and interaction

Use Atkinson Hyperlegible with a system-sans fallback. Black display headlines, readable text measure, 44px minimum touch targets, visible focus states, native disclosure controls, and only non-essential motion.
