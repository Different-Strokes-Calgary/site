# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Calgary LGBTQ+ masters swimmers and allies looking for an inclusive club, current practice details, and a clear route to membership.

## Product Purpose

Explain who Different Strokes Calgary is, when and where it swims, and how prospective members can join. Success means a visitor can confidently find the current schedule and membership path without needing to navigate separate pages.

## Brand Personality

Joyful, capable, welcoming.

## Anti-references

Generic sports-club templates, rainbow interface chrome, dense administrative portals, and decorative card grids.

## Design Principles

- Let the club's original illustrated community carry personality.
- Put current practice information before supporting detail.
- Make joining feel clear, never salesy.
- Keep the public site easy to scan and easy to use by keyboard.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.2 AA. Respect reduced motion, use semantic disclosure controls, maintain high contrast, and use inclusive language throughout.
